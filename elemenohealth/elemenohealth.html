Elemeno Health

Duration

Fall 2020 (6 weeks)

My role

UX designer (contract)


Elemeno Health is platform for hospitals to improve the practice and delivery of medical resources and healthcare technology. The product looks into the problem space for nurse managers to easily allocate these materials to other medical staff.

My role


The client approached me with a clear problem that nurse managers had difficult finding important functions within their dashboard. I was contracted to look into the information architecture and UI design in order to optimize the product for access to medical resources.
